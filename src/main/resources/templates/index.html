Schedule Events

Keep the family up to date easily on who, what, where, and when by scheduling events for them to see! Each family has their own agenda they can share to always be able to be at the right place at the right time.

Assign Tasks

Have the ability to assign and recieve tasks with family members so that you don't forget that thing your spouse asked you to pick up on the way home from work! Family members will be able to see what tasks are done, in progress, or have yet to be started so that everyone will be in the know.

Preserve History

Comment and upload images for upcoming and past events so that you can always look back and remember what a fun time the family had! Coming together to share your experiences and have visuals for an event or task gives your family a better way to remember!
